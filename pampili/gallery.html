Tracker

Tracker is a GPS tracking app, which tracks a configured GPS device. User can create Geo-Fences, track devices, see the history of trips, analytics, get alarms if out of geo-fence, get push notifications, share with other users. User can tag a geo-fence to a device and get notifications to user if the device goes out of geo-fence. I developed the complete application from scratch. It provides comfort and security to those who need to know the location of their valuable things, vehicles, trucks, cattle, pets and kids.
