Favorite Video Games

My top five.

Minecraft

Minecraft is one of those games that needs no introduction, everyone has heard, seen, or played it. There were many other games that I played as a child, but Minecraft will always be one that I can come back to and have the same amount of fun as I would have all those years ago.

The Binding of Isaac

This game, I assume, is not one that most people know about. In my opinion, there are a number of words that can be used to describe this game, such as: fun, unique, challenging, roguelike, indie, action-adventure, and more.

Halo Reach

For me, this was/is the best game that has come out of the Halo series (although, Halo 2, 3, and Infinite are good, too). The campaign and custimization in the multiplayer mode was/is amazing, which is also why I like the new Halo game, Halo Infinite.

Apex Legends

In my opinion, Apex Legends is the best (not in terms of success) battle royale game to date. I love the mechanics of the game and the different abilites on each character makes it way more fun as well. Though it can be very frustrating at times, I always have a good time playing Apex with my friends.

SSB Ultimate

This game is one that comes from a long-running series called Super Smash Bros. I grew up playing the older games, so I have a sort of soft spot for them. They are always very fun to play with friends or bring to parties/get-togethers (which is something that I often do). It is a 2D, crossover fighting game with characters from various games that everyone will know (Mario, Luigi, Bowser, etc.).
