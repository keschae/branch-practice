Education

When I first started going to ISU, I was an Actuarial Science major. I realized during my junior year that this wasn't what I wanted to do. I decided to make the switch to Statistics because I wanted to make sure I still graduated on time. During my Senior year, I finally stopped and thought about what I actually wanted to do with my life. I had taken IT 168 my freshman year as a requirement for my major and enjoyed it a lot. I have always loved solving problems and coming up with solutions to them. I realized that coding was what I wanted to do, and so I made a switch to Computer Science and I am very happy that I did.
